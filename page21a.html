Frames

To get a subtle look:

Face Shape:

· The rounder your face, the squarer the frame.

· The squarer your face, the rounder the frame.

• The longer your face, the deeper the frame.

· If you have a broader forehead than your cheeks, go for frames with heavier bottoms.

· If you have wider cheeks than forehead, go for frames with heavier tops.

 

Colour:

· If you have a light skin tone go for light colours like silver, blue and pink.

· If you have a warm skin tone, go for warmer colours like brown, gold and red

· If you have a darker skin complexion black frames will blend in.

· Beware of choosing a colour similar to your hair as this colour will dominate your face

 

Materials:

· Lightest materials are titanium and stainless steel.

· Nickel alloy frames are light, but they may react with skin if you have an allergy to cheap jewellery. Stainless steel frames may have nickel alloy in them

· Plastic frames are bold. They are good if you have thick lenses as they hide the thickness. They also hold the lens in place more securely as is a mould, not relying in screws to hold frame together.

 

Frame Style:

· Rimless – most discreet especially if you have a frame made of thin titanium

· Semi rimless – very discreet – especially if you choose a colour that blends with your skin tone. You can have a bit more fun with the colour of the frame as it will not dominate your face.

· Full rim – this frame will suit all lenses.

 

Bolder Look:

Go crazy – big, plastic, in any colour or shape.
